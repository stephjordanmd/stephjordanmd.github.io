📌 The Normalization Question — ₱12.63/share difference
The entire BUY vs DON'T BUY split comes down to one 73-basis-point judgment: what is the risk-free rate? Damodaran: use today's rate — it reflects real market conditions. Duff & Phelps: normalize — today's rates are a COVID distortion. Given AP's essential-service status (fully operational through ECQ), PRS Aaa bond rating, 50:50 Cleanergy target, and utility-grade cash flow stability, the as-is WACC with a BUY recommendation is defensible — provided the analyst acknowledges the rate normalization risk explicitly.
